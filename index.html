Who We Are

About C3

Bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world industry skills.

🎯

Our Mission

To help CSE students bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry skills through sessions, collaboration and practical exposure.

🚀

Our Vision

To build a strong tech community where students continuously learn, share knowledge and grow towards successful careers in the industry.

🤝

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork. C3 fosters an environment of peer learning, open knowledge sharing and inclusive growth for all members.

💡

Innovation

From hackathons to AI workshops, we keep our members at the cutting edge of technology trends and industry practices.
